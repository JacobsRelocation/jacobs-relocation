HeyGen workflow (when ready)
One-time setup, then weekly content production in minutes
1
Sign up & train your avatar
Record 2-3 minutes of yourself talking to camera in a quiet, well-lit space. HeyGen needs about 5 minutes to train a custom avatar. Subscription: ~$24/month (Creator plan).
2
Test on Instagram first
Before building a whole workflow, generate one test Reel and check how it looks on mobile at 9:16. Some avatars read uncanny on small screens. If it works → continue.
3
Build your script template library
Create 3-4 reusable script templates: "Market update this week", "Cost comparison: X vs Florida", "Visa explainer", "Neighbourhood breakdown". Each one is just a fill-in-the-blanks.
4
Connect to Market Intel briefing
Each Monday, run your Market Intel briefing → take the talking points → feed into the "Market update" template → HeyGen generates a 60-second avatar Reel ready to post. 10 minutes total.
5
Mix with personal content
Aim for roughly 40% HeyGen (informational), 60% you-on-camera (personal/lifestyle). The mix is what stops the avatar feeling robotic — your real face still dominates the grid.
