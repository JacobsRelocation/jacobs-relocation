Strategy note
US domestic movers are a frictionless audience — no visa complexity, no currency risk, no international school anxiety. They need the lifestyle case made clearly and someone they trust to handle logistics. Your San Francisco background is a powerful credibility signal for West Coast movers specifically. This pillar also avoids the obstacles international clients face and opens up a significantly larger addressable market.
